About me

I am currently a postdoctoral researcher at the Space Telescope Science Institute (STScI). My research mainly focuses around massive stars, understanding the physics of their winds and the impact on stellar evolution and feedback to their host galaxy. Currently, I am interested in starburst galaxies and have been working on determining their integrated properties and developing synthetic observations.
